Marriages

You can marry player using GUI section Marriage. Here you click on compass icon Find Someone to Marry, type player's name. If player is not online or doesnt exist, marriage will be automatically canceled. If player is online, they get notified.

Marriage accepting/denying

For example you sent proposal to PlayerB, once this player was notified, they can either deny or accept your request through GUI section Active Proposals.

Divorce

You can divorce your partner through the Marriage menu. You need permission valentines.divorce, to do that.

Toggle marriages

Player can disable marriages through settings.

Anniversaries

Plugin automatically tracks how long couples have been married. Anniversary broadcasts include yearly anniversaries.

Statistics

Each marriage stores partner, marriage date, and days married.

Why you can't marry

Any of you is already married; you propose to yourself; player disabled marriage requests; you have active proposal; youre on cooldown
